"Dialogue, dialogue, dialogue. That is how you learn"
"Even though we live in illusions, we must work hard to fulfill our illusions as best as we can." - John B. Goodenough 

There is nothing more enlightening than having meaningful discussions with individuals who have a different perspective than yours. 

Character is built through daily tensions, knowledge through interaction, understanding through revision. Putting your thoughts into
into written form forces precision, clarity and purpose. Without communication, through the sciences - written or spoken - arts or otherwise, 
your ideas will remain as such - just ideas.
