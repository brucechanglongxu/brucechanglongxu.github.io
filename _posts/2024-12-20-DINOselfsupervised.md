DINO, short for _Self-Distillation with No Labels_, is a self-supervised learning approach designed for Vision Transformers (ViTs). Unlike traditional convolutional neural networks (CNNs), ViTs process images with a transformer-based backbone. DINO takes this a step further by eliminating the need for labeled datasets, instead leveraging self-distillation to train models that can learn meaningful representations on their own.
