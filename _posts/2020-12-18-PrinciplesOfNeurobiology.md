This class is taught and largely based of Liqun Luo's seminal "Principles of Neurobiology". The following is an outline of the chapter syllabi and this blog post seeks to break down the topics further and hopefully offer some insight why this field is so incredibly rich and fulfilling. 

Lecture 1: Invitation to Neurobiology \\
Lecture 2: Signalling Within Neurons \\
Lecture 3-4: Signalling Across Synapses \\
Lecture 5-7: Vision \\
Lecture 8-9: Olfaction, Taste, Audition and Somatosensation \\
Lecture 10: Sexual Behaviour \\
Lecture 11: Memory, Learning and Synaptic Plasticity \\
Lecture 12-13: Brain Disorders  \\
Lecture 14-15: Evolution of the Nervous System \\
Lecture 16: Ways of Exploring
