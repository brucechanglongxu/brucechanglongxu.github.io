This was a paper published in the New England Journal of Medicine by Harold J. Burnstein MD, PhD which nicely summarises the state of the art for the most widely found form of breast cancer. This blog post will attempt to summarise the key points of the paper, and suggest future directions in which scientists should start thinking.
