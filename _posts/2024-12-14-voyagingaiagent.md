VOYAGER is the first LLM-driven embodied lifelong learning agent, designed to continuously explore, develop new skills, and adapt to novel challenges without human intervention. Unlike traditional reinforcement learning or imitation learning methods, which operate on primitive actions, VOYAGER harnesses the power of large language models (LLMs) like GPT-4 to facilitate high-level planning and skill acquisition.
