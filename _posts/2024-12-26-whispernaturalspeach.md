The field of speech recognition has witnessed rapid progress, particularly with unsupervised pre-training methods such as Wav2Vec 2.0. However, these methods often require fine-tuning, limiting their broad applicability. This blog post delves into an innovative approach that leverages large-scale weak supervision to train speech recognition models that generalize across multiple languages and tasks without dataset-specific fine-tuning.
