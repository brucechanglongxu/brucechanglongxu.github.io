Mathematical problem-solving is inherently structured and logic-driven, demanding precise reasoning and step-by-step deduction. DeepSeekMath 7B builds upon the DeepSeek-Coder-Base-v1.5 7B model, integrating a massive 120 billion math-related tokens sourced from Common Crawl, as well as natural language and code data. Achieving an impressive 51.7% accuracy on the MATH benchmark, DeepSeekMath surpasses all other open-source models while maintaining a relatively compact architecture.
