The intersection of artificial general intelligence, drug discovery, and biotechnology is rapidly transforming how we develop and deliver life-saving therapies. As someone deeply involved in AI infrastructure, computational drug discovery, and biotech strategy, my career has naturally evolved at the crossroads of biopharma innovation and AI-driven healthcare solutions.From my work at Cerebras Systems, NVIDIA, and Apple in high-performance AI infrastructure to my collaborations with Stanford Medicine, Mayo Clinic, and global pharmaceutical leaders (GSK, NovoNordisk, AstraZeneca), I’ve seen firsthand how cutting-edge AI can reshape industries. My research and partnerships have focused on accelerating precision oncology, immunology, metabolic disorders, and cardiovascular therapeutics — a mission that continues to shape my long-term trajectory.

This post outlines the vision behind Panacea Plexus, a blueprint for where I see my career heading: integrating AI, biotech, and executive leadership to drive impact at scale. My goal is to build on experiences across AI infrastructure, pharma R&D, and venture partnerships to lead transformative change in oncology, immunology, metabolic medicine, and cardiovascular disease research.
