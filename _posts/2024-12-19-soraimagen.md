Sora is the latest milestone in the evolution of generative artificial intelligence. Unlike its predecessors, which were limited to producing short, often disjointed video clips, Sora leverages diffusion transformers to generate longer, more coherent, and visually intricate scenes. This achievement builds upon earlier advances in text-to-image models, such as DALL·E and Stable Diffusion, extending AI’s generative prowess into the temporal dimension. At its core, Sora employs a diffusion transformer architecture, similar to large-scale language models like GPT-4, but adapted for video synthesis.
