---
title: Take Ownership of your Profession
layout: post
---

Didi Chuxing's president Jean Liu perspective on competition is something that I support highly. She aptly states that competition is a blessing and an opportunity to be better:

"When you have competition, you're in a hurdles game; you just need to focus. You don't keep looking back, looking aside - you move on. You just keep looking at where you want to go."

Competition does indeed bring out the best in us and I love it. Take ownership of your professional career, the way you brand yourself will dictate the type of opportunities that you will be exposed towards. The way you carry yourself - think, act and speak - will be how the world views, respects or direspects you. The way you spend your time and the habits that you form will determine your future, every second is precious and needs to be spent well - money can be regained but time cannot.
