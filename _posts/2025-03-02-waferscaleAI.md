Large AI requires large infrastructure, and wafer-scale processors are optimized for maximum dataflow execution, SIMD-heavy parallelism, and efficient inter-tile communication. Each **processing element** is the fundamental compute unit in Heisenberg, where a single PE is responsible for executing SIMD operations, managing wavelets, and handling local data storage.
