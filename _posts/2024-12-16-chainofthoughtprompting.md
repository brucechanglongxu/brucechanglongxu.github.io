As large language models (LLMs) grow more powerful, their reasoning capabilities remain a key area of improvement. Chain of Thought (CoT) prompting is a groundbreaking technique that enhances LLMs' ability to tackle complex problems step by step, mirroring human reasoning.By explicitly guiding the model to break down a problem into logical intermediate steps, CoT prompting significantly improves performance in math reasoning, commonsense reasoning, and multi-hop question answering. In this post, we’ll explore how Chain of Thought prompting works, why it outperforms standard prompting, and how it’s shaping the future of AI-driven reasoning.
