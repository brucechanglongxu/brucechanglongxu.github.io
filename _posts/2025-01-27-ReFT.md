_ReFT (Representation Fine-Tuning)_ flips the fine-tuning playbook on its head by focusing on hidden representations instead of weights. It introduces a paradigm that is lightweight, efficient, and powerful -- delivering state-of-the-art results without the parameter bloat of traditional methods. Let's break down why ReFT is a game changer. 

Traditional approaches like LoRA update weights, but ReFT directly modifies the hidden representations during forward passes. This eliminates the need for extensive parameter updates, making the method far more efficient whilst retaining task specificity. It achieves 15-65 times more parameter efficiency compared to methods like LoRA, whilst outperforming or matching their results.
