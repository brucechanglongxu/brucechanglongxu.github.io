About Me

I’m Bruce Changlong Xu, a researcher and aspiring physician-scientist dedicated to bridging human sensory perception with artificial intelligence. My work focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technologies in computer vision and AI to advance fields like ophthalmology, radiology, and oncology.

Currently, I am honing my skills at Cerebras Systems, while preparing for an MD-PhD journey to develop innovative solutions that transform health through semantic perception.

My Vision

My North Star is "Semantic Perception": interpreting visual, sensory, and molecular data to enable proactive, tech-augmented clinical care, enhancing early disease detection in oncology, cardiovascular, and metabolic diseases.
